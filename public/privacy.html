Pridebot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2024

1. Data Collection

Pridebot collects the following data:

  • User IDs: For server logging, command logging, and profile purposes.
  • Server IDs: For server logging and command logging.

2. Use of Data

The collected data is used for security and development purposes. This includes:

  • Monitoring and ensuring the security of the services.
  • Improving the features and functionality of Pridebot.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or to respond to legal processes.
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users and third parties.

4. Data Security

All collected IDs are securely stored in MongoDB. We implement reasonable measures to protect the security of your data. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Data Deletion

If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact Sdriver1 or in the Support Server. We will process your request and delete your data from our records.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Your continued use of Pridebot after any changes will constitute your acceptance of the new terms.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Sdriver1 or ask for help in the Support Server.
